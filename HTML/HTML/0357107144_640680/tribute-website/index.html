Shigetaka Kurita

The Creator of Emojis

Shigetaka Kurita is a Japanese interface designer and often cited for his early work with emoji sets. Many refer to him as the creator of the emoji, a claim clarified in recent years. His innovation would be intepreted by Google and Apple, and integrated into their keyboards for widespread, universal communication.
